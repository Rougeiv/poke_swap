How to Play

Your goal is to collect ALL the Pokemons!

You must create an account before participating in trades!

Don't have an account? Sign up here!


1. Players obtain Pokemons by trading with other players or by catching it themselves for 1 or 10 tokens.

2. Catching a Pokemon you already own will reduce the amount of tokens you have by 1.

3. Players are first given 10 free tokens upon signup. Players are awarded 3 tokens upon posting a trade and 6 tokens when accepting a trade.

4. Players may only post 3 trade requests at a time.

5. Complete your collection of Pokemon and have fun!
