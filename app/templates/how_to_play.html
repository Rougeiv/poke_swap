How to Play

Your goal is to catch ALL the pokemon!

You must create an account before participating in trades!

Don't have an account? Sign up here!


1. Daily logins provide you with PokeTokens that you can use to catch Pokemon. PokeTokens may also be obtained via credit card information.

2. Offer trade requests with other players to collect other Pokemon.

3. You can only put up to 3 trade requests at a time.

4. Complete your collection of Pokemon and have fun!
